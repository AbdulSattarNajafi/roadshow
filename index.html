Welcome to the Sangfor 2024 Roadshow!

SYNERGAIZE Your Digital Transformation – is the theme for this year’s Sangfor Roadshow 2024. We have talked before about Synergy across Sangfor solutions paving the road for smarter, simpler digital transformation. Still, many users face challenges like complex IT management, escalating security threats, and rising costs. But by enhancing Synergy with Sangfor’s new AI capabilities, these challenges can be met head-on and bulldozed out of your way as you move towards transforming your environment in any way you need.

Join us for this year’s Roadshow to experience the transformative power of AI merged into Sangfor’s innovative technologies. Highlights include SecurityGPT, a security-centric chatbot that redefines threat detection and response. Witness fully automated security operations & investigation, smarter targeted response recommendations, and intelligent malware sample creation for proactive defense testing. Complementing this is Sangfor SkyOps, our new AIOps platform that revolutionizes HCI management with automated data-driven workflows.

The event will also showcase Sangfor XDDR 2.0—our next generation security framework, featuring a deeper and richer integration of Platforms, Products, and Services, enabling powerful new solutions like 3 Seconds to Kill Ransomware. Our Unified Hybrid Cloud further embodies Synergy by merging on-premises Sangfor HCI Private Cloud with HCI-powered Sangfor Managed Cloud, ensuring a cohesive and resilient IT ecosystem that meets your digital business needs.

SYNERGAIZE Your Digital Transformation at Sangfor Roadshow 2024 – where AI meets Synergy to empower Your Digital Success.

Speakers

Nicholas Tay Chee Seng

Sangfor Cloud Chief Product Officer

Nicholas has an extensive career spanning over 20 years, during which he has played a major role in the ideation, formulation, and co-creation of new cloud solutions for both enterprise and government sectors. His proficiency is backed by various sales and pre-sales certifications from key global technology firms such as Sangfor, AWS, Microsoft Azure, Dell EMC, Google, IBM, NetApp, Oracle, Red Hat, and VMware, enabling him to adopt the latest global technology trends, especially in the cloud domains.

Guy​ Rosefelt

Sangfor Chief Product Officer

Guy joined Sangfor in 2019 as our Chief Marketing Officer for Security. Previously serving as the Director of Threat Intelligence and Web Security Product Management for NSFOCUS and with over 30 years of experience, Guy is a leader and groundbreaker at the crux of a dynamic cybersecurity industry.

Tian Koon Lee

Sangfor Cloud Product Director

Tian Koon - aka TK - joined Sangfor in 2014 and has held various positions in the company as a Pre-Sales Director and Country Manager in Singapore and Korea. TK has over 10 years of experience supplying IT solutions and expert advice - which allowed him to develop a broad set of expertise that he uses to develop customer-centric solutions.
